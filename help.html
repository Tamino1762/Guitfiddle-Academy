Welcome To The Help Center!

What is this, you ask? This is designed to help current Guitfiddle Academy students make the most of thier music education with online tools. Many of these have already been suggested in lessons, but now they're all in one place! Also, some have been/will be added as they are found, so check back often! For the parents, there are also helpful suggestions and links so you can help your child be successful.
